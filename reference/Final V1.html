Leadership tracks

One foundation. Four ways to lead.

Everyone builds a complete understanding of the diagnostic business — then goes deep in the track matched to their strengths.

Track 01

Sales Leadership

Drive revenue across diagnostic product lines. Build the territory strategy, the B2B relationships, and the clinical fluency that earns trust with lab directors and pathologists.

Grows into: Regional Sales Manager → Sales Director
What you build
Territory & account strategy
B2B relationship management
Clinical product positioning
Diagnostic sales metrics
Track 02

Operations Leadership

Run the engine room. Manage lab workflows, resource planning, and operational efficiency across diagnostic networks — the backbone of every high-performing organisation.

Grows into: Operations Manager → Operations Head / COO
What you build
Lab workflow & TAT management
Resource & capacity planning
SOP development
Cost & efficiency control
Track 03

Quality Assurance

Lead quality systems, accreditation, and regulatory compliance. The function that keeps diagnostic companies trusted, audit-ready, and ahead of standards.

Grows into: QA Lead → Head of Quality & Compliance
What you build
NABL & ISO frameworks
Internal audit preparation
Quality KPI management
Corrective action protocols
Track 04

Product Management

Shape diagnostic and IVD product strategy, manage lifecycles, and bridge the clinical and commercial sides of the business — the fastest-growing role in the industry.

Grows into: Product Manager → Head of Product
What you build
Product positioning & strategy
Market & competitor analysis
IVD application training
Commercial launch frameworks
